Now leasing: an entire city

Welcome to Line City.

A hand-drawn skyline at the end of a long red road — two-point perspective, notebook-paper streets, weather included at no extra charge. You start with $50 and a duplex that needs some love. You end with a monument to greed visible from space.

Rent fills a bar. You tap the bar. The number goes up, and up, and — this is the part our lawyers love — it never has to come down. RENT RAISER is an idle landlord tycoon that mocks you the entire time you're winning.
